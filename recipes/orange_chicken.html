Orange Chicken

Description

This 4-ingredient orange chicken made with frozen chicken tenders is great for when you're craving takeout.

It's so simple to make at home, is very budget-friendly, and only takes 4 ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup orange marmalade
  • 1/2 cup Kansas city-style BBQ sauce
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 bag frozen fully cooked chicken nuggets
  • Sliced green onions (optional)
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 Degrees F. Place frozen nuggets in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and crispy, 11 to 13 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, whisk marmalade, BBQ sauce and soy sauce together in a small saucepan and heat over low heat until hot, for 5 minutes
  4. Place nuggets in a large bowl. Drizzle sauce over the top. Toss to coat.
