Hotdog Roll

Description

Just when you thought hot dogs couldn't get more fun, here's a tasty new twist.

Paired with the creamy mayo dip, they're irresistible for kids and adults alike.

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices white bread.
  • 8 beef hotdogs.
  • 2 eggs.
  • A pack of breadcrumb.

Steps:

>
  1. Using a rolling pin, roll each bread flat.
  2. Place 1 hot dog horizontally to each bread.
  3. Roll the bread and apply pressure at the edge to seal the bread together.
  4. Place the hotdog roll into a bowl of egg. Then roll it in a plate of breadcrumb.
  5. Deep fry the hotdog roll with a medium heat. Serve the hotdog roll with mayonnaise for a dip.
