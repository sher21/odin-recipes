Lasagna

Description

Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn't have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

An easy lasagna recipe that will saves you lots of time.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cook and drain ground beef, then stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cheese, eggs and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna.
  4. Bake, covered for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
